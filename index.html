Procedural Animation Animals

A collection of animals simulated in Python using various procedural animation techniques. Each animal is composed of a number of constrained circles that are linked in a chain to simulate natural movement. The legs use inverse kinematics to replicate the way the limbs would move in real life.
This project has given me a great understanding of procedural animation and I hope to use it for another project some day!

FTC 2024 - CENTERSTAGE

As a project for my Gr. 12 Computer Engineering course, my team built a robot to compete in the 2024 FTC season. As the sole programmer, I developed the driver-operated functionality, and constructed a complete autonomous routine that implemented image recognition using TensorFlow to identify and orient itself to AprilTags and other game objects. This autonomous routine allowed us to win a district competition and end the season as a provincial team captain, one of only 4 in the entire province.
